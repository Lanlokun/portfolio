I am a Research Engineer working at the frontier of AI and Computational Neuroscience. 我是一名工作在人工智能与计算神经科学前沿领域的研究工程师。

Currently at Nankai University, I am building the Universal Narrative Engine. My work involves decoding latent neural states from the Alice Dataset. 目前就读于南开大学，我正在开发通用叙事引擎 (UNE)。我的研究重点是解码 Alice 数据集 中的潜在大脑状态。

Beyond the lab, find me on the football pitch, at the billiards table, or playing ping pong. I believe research is fueled by a spicy plate of Gong Bao chicken in Tianjin or the comforting taste of West African staples from home. 在实验室之外，你会发现我在足球场驰骋，或在台球桌和乒乓球台前竞技。我深信，研究的灵感源自天津地道的宫保鸡丁，亦或是家乡西非主食的亲切滋味。
